Site Plan: Kings Gaming Store

Site Name

Kings Gaming Store

This name was selected because the site focuses on selling and recommending popular video games curated by “Kings” editors and the gaming community. It conveys authority and fun in the gaming space.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for gamers to discover, purchase, and learn about trending video games. It highlights featured titles, offers pricing details, and connects users with curated recommendations from the community.

Scenarios

  • Which are the top-selling games right now and how much do they cost?
  • Where can I find details about a specific game, such as genre and price?
  • How do I purchase a featured game directly from the site?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Dark Blue (#2c3e50) – used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: Light Gray (#ecf0f1) – used for backgrounds and wireframe areas.

Accent Color: Bright Red (#e74c3c) – used for buttons or purchase highlights.

Typography

Heading Font: Verdana – clear and bold for section titles.

Body Font: Arial – simple and readable for paragraphs and descriptions.

Accent Font: Georgia – optional for featured quotes or highlighted text.
